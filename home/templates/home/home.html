About Us

Know More About HOPE Driving School

Who are we?

HOPE Driving School is a leading driving school in the city. We offer complete driving courses at the best affordable rates. Our skilled and devoted instructors are able to offer the best services to bring out competent drivers that comply with our road regulations and guidelines

Why choose us?

We offer assessment tests, flexible programmes to suit your schedules, new cars, and unlimited theory lessons. We do train with the latest NTSA curriculum to ensure we all come together to promote road safety in Kenya. With prior arrangement, we also do pick and/or drop our students.
